About Study Sage

Study Sage is a project created by a group of teenagers from Imagine Scholar, a non-profit organization located in Nkomazi aimed at creating a network of extraordinary thinkers and doers who solve interesting problem.

We have identified that many of our peers in high school are not perfoming well academically, and one of the reasons could be due to the lack of exposure to different learning strategies that could help during the learning/studying process. With that we created Study Sage, a website where our peers could easily access and be equipped with learning strategies.
